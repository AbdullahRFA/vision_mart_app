1. Introduction

The Context: Bangladesh produces over 1 million tons of mangoes annually. Indigenous varieties like Amrapali and Harivanga are economically vital but visually similar.

The Problem: Manual grading is error-prone. Existing Deep Learning models (VGG16, ResNet) are too computationally expensive for the low-cost smartphones used by rural farmers.

Objective: To benchmark MobileNetV3 (Proposed) against MobileNetV2 (Baseline) for high-accuracy, offline classification on edge devices.
